Sleep is a Necessity

"A good laugh and a long sleep are the best cures in the doctor's book." – Irish Proverb

Sleep is essential for physical health, emotional well-being, and cognitive function. Adults typically need 7–9 hours of sleep per night to maintain optimal health. Proper sleep helps memory, improves mood, and reduces stress levels, while chronic sleep deprivation can lead to serious health issues.
