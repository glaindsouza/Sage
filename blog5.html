Understanding Emotional Burnout

Rest isn’t laziness — it’s recovery 💤

Emotional burnout happens when we give more than we have to offer...

💬 Signs of Burnout

Constant exhaustion, lack of motivation, or irritability are not weakness — they are signals.

🌤️ How to Heal

Rest, say no, reconnect with what brings joy. Healing starts with slowing down.

💫 You Deserve Peace

Burnout isn’t the end — it’s your body asking for compassion.
