Self-Love Isn’t Selfish

Because caring for yourself is caring for the world 🌸

We often give love freely to others but forget to offer the same compassion to ourselves...

💖 Know Your Worth

You are enough — even when you are still healing. Loving yourself is the first step to lasting happiness.

🌈 Set Boundaries

Boundaries protect your peace and remind you that your energy is valuable.

☀️ Practice Kindness

Speak to yourself the way you’d comfort a friend. That’s true self-care.
